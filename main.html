About Us

ABC Painting Contractor is a family-owned and operated painting company serving the local community for over 10 years. We take pride in our work and are committed to delivering high-quality results that exceed our clients' expectations.

Our Services

Interior Painting

We specialize in interior painting for residential and commercial properties. Our team of skilled painters uses only premium quality paints and materials to ensure the longevity and durability of our work.

Exterior Painting

We offer exterior painting services to give your home or business a fresh new look. Our painters are experienced in handling all types of surfaces and use only high-quality paints and materials to ensure the longevity and durability of our work.

Power Washing

We also offer power washing services to clean and restore your home's exterior surfaces. Our team uses state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to remove dirt, grime, and stains from a variety of surfaces, including siding, decks, and patios.
